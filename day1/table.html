Idli

Idli is a traditional South Indian dish made from fermented rice and lentil batter, steamed into soft, fluffy cakes. It's often served with coconut chutney and sambar, a spicy lentil soup.

Idli and Vada are iconic breakfast staples and cherished elements of the famous food of Tamil Nadu. Idli, a soft, steamed rice cake, and Vada, a crispy, Savory lentil doughnut, are often enjoyed for their wholesome and delicious combination.
