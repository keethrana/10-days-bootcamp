WELCOME TO JMS !

JMS Mining Private Limited, hereafter referred as JMS, is the pioneer & market leader in implementation of Mass Production Technology in Underground Coal Mines. Since our inception in 2007, we have been a prominent service provider in Underground Mining Industry. The company has implemented world’s latest technology – Continuous Miner & Bolter Miner in this country. It has presence in major Underground coal mining clusters in the country, namely at BCCL, ECL, CCL, WCL. The company has also executed work for Singareni Collieries Company Ltd., India’s top two coal producing companies, are its esteemed clients.

We have introduced Bolter Miner first time in India. We have introduced system for Real Time Prediction of roof caving in developing operation. We maintain our assets by following the best practices in the Industry. We do our everyday activities at all places and levels. Our Life Cycle Management for underground mines is the best in its class. We manufacture support materials to maintain a safe working environment at the mines and ensure their easy availability of the same.

We have owned three Commercial Coal Blocks in the auction organised by MoC. We have bagged two MDO projects in CCL & ECL. Also, JMS received an award for 'Best Employer' in Manufacturing Workplace for FY 2022-23 presented by Marksmen Daily and India Today TV Initiative.

Our Expertise

JMS offers multifarious and of superior quality mining services – Geo-technical investigations, mine planning and designing, preparation of detailed project reports, mine operation, development of mines related infrastructures, supply of state-of-the-art mining equipment are major of them. Its strata control & monitoring system are of world class level. EHS Management System is as per national and global norms.

Our People & Foundation

At JMS, the driving philosophy behind their service is 'quality begets quality'. The enterprise has an army of competent, technically qualified people, minimum qualification being ITI. Talent, imbued with responsibility, coupled with continuous empowerment and leadership. Apart from a talented team, what sets JMS apart from its contemporaries is its network of reputed manufacturers and consultants. Our invaluable human resource, coupled with our management principles, policies, is integral to JMS' steady rise in the nation's energy sector.
